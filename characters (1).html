Meet the Characters
of Zoofrinsville

From the pages of The One & Only Magical Tooth Fairy — eleven unforgettable friends with one very important question.

🦁 Lion

Larry Lion

The brave cub who started it all.

Little Larry Lion lost his first tooth and set off the greatest mystery in Zoofrinsville. Curious, enthusiastic, and refreshingly honest, Larry isn't afraid to admit when he's confused — which, on this particular day, is quite a lot.

"I know one thing, the Tooth Fairy can't be a hamster AND a bunny."
CuriousBraveHonestExcitable

✦ Signature Look

  • Blue shirt
  • Lost tooth (precious!)
🐹 Hamster

Henry Hamster

Small in size, big in confidence.

Henry is Zoofrinsville's most enthusiastic — if somewhat breathless — little hamster. He speaks in stutters and stammers but never in doubt, especially when it comes to his firm belief that the Tooth Fairy is most definitely, positively, absolutely a hamster.

"The T-T-Tooth Fairy is most d-definitely a hamster!"
ConfidentBreathlessLoyalStubborn

✦ Signature Look

  • Teal striped shirt
🐰 Bunny

Clarissa Bunny

She knows a lot about everything.

Clarissa is the fastest thing in Zoofrinsville — both on her feet and in her words. She's clever, opinionated, and absolutely certain that the Tooth Fairy is a bunny. She's somebody, and everybody knows she knows a lot about everything.

"The Tooth Fairy is the most magical, magnificent bunny in all the land!"
QuickCleverTalkativeOpinionated

✦ Signature Look

  • Pink tracksuit
  • Racing stripes
🐢 Twin Turtles

Taylor & Torrie Turtle

The brainiest duo in the library.

Taylor and Torrie are the twin turtles who know more about most things than most people know about just about anything. Surrounded by towers of books and dressed identically with roundish glasses, one speaks at normal turtle speed — the other even... slower.

"Pure...gold...24...carat...shines...like...the...sun."
BookishMethodicalWiseSlow-talking

✦ Signature Look

  • Matching glasses
  • Towers of books
  • Teal shells
🐨 Koala

Dreamy Koala

22 hours asleep, 2 hours of wisdom.

Dreamy the Koala sleeps twenty-two hours a day, which gives her plenty of time to dream — and plenty of dreams to know things others don't. When she finally wakes, her answers arrive wrapped in poetry and wonder. Today's answer: the Tooth Fairy is a koala.

"Why, what a surprise, so many friends, so many eyes!"
DreamyPoeticSleepyMystical

✦ Signature Look

  • Striped pajamas
  • Hammock
  • Flowers in hair
🦧 Orangutan

Fenwick the Orangutan

Artist. Visionary. Very dramatic.

Fenwick is Zoofrinsville's resident artist — fiery orange fur, red beret, and a studio full of paint cans and grand pronouncements. Under the solemn oath of artistry, he declares the Tooth Fairy to be a tremendously fabulous orangutan with wings like emeralds and a ruby red wand.

"...a ruby red wand that turns teeth into diamonds! Or coins, or biscuits."
CreativeDramaticExpressiveGenerous

✦ Signature Look

  • Red beret
  • Paint cans
  • Easel & canvas
🦘 Kangaroo

Shuffles the Kangaroo

Tappity tap — he never stops dancing.

Shuffles the Kangaroo makes every entrance an event — tappity tap, tappity tap across the hardwood floor. He's joyful, light on his feet, and absolutely convinced the Tooth Fairy is the most beautiful, magical kangaroo ever known.

"Tonight you will receive a visit from the Tooth Fairy, the most beautiful, magical kangaroo ever known!"
EnergeticJoyfulRhythmicShowman

✦ Signature Look

  • Tap shoes
  • Black dance outfit
👑 Hippopotamus

Princess the Hippopotamus

Grace. Poise. More princessious than anyone.

Princess the Hippopotamus is Zoofrinsville's prima ballerina — always in her pink tutu and pointe shoes, always mid-pirouette. She carries herself with breathtaking grace and an equally breathtaking level of confidence, complete with a small golden crown she has very much earned.

"The Tooth Fairy is a wondrous, glorious, more princessious hippopotamus than I!"
GracefulRegalConfidentDramatic

✦ Signature Look

  • Gold crown
  • Pink tutu
  • Pointe shoes
🦉 Owl

Mayor Owl

Whooooo knows best? He does.

Mayor Owl sits regally perched in his great redwood City Hall, overlooking Zoofrinsville with enormous round glasses and a measured sense of authority. He speaks in long, careful sentences about things you know, things you think you know, things you believe — and things you imagine.

"Whooooo is the Tooth Fairy? That is an age old question, my fellow citizens."
WiseMeasuredAuthoritativePhilosophical

✦ Signature Look

  • Round glasses
  • Blue suit
  • Bow tie
  • Redwood City Hall
🦄 Unicorn

The Unicorn

The mythical truth-teller of Zoofrinsville.

No one had ever seen a unicorn before — most of them believed in unicorns, but that's another story. She arrived at just the right moment, clapping her hooves to silence the debate, and in the sweetest voice anyone had ever heard, revealed the truth about the One and Only Magical Tooth Fairy.

"The Tooth Fairy is not a lion, hamster, bunny, turtle, koala, orangutan, kangaroo, hippopotamus nor is she an owl."
MysticalGracefulWiseMagical

✦ Signature Look

  • Flower crown
  • Pink mane
  • Silver horn
🧚 The One & Only

The Tooth Fairy

She's the One and Only Magical Tooth Fairy.

She is the One and Only Magical Tooth Fairy — for all the creatures on earth, for the sharks in the sea, for the tigers and the turtles and the lions. Within her powers is the mystical power to turn a missing tooth into a world of imagination, where anything and everything is possible.

"She's the One and Only, Magical Tooth Fairy!"
MagicalRadiantMysticalWondrous

✦ Signature Look

  • Teal hair braid
  • Gold crown
  • Purple wings
  • Wand with tooth
