The One & Only Magical Tooth Fairy

📖 48 Pages 🎨 Fully Illustrated 👶 Ages 4–8 🏡 Bedtime Favorite

When the Tooth Fairy visits Zoofrinville for the very first time, every Zoofrin learns that even life's smallest moments — a tiny tooth, a small act of kindness — can hold the biggest magic. A warm, funny, and genuinely moving story about growing up, letting go, and the adventures that come next.

What's Inside

Everything a Great Children's Book Should Be

Zoofrins books are built to delight kids and create conversation — with themes that matter and illustrations that spark imagination.

🎨

Rich Illustrations

Full-color artwork on every spread brings Zoofrinville to life — each character rendered in the warm, rounded style that defines the brand.

💛

Emotional Themes

Real feelings, handled with care. Every Zoofrins story helps kids process big emotions — change, courage, friendship — through adventure.

😂

Genuine Humor

Jokes that land for kids and fly right over their heads in the best possible way. Parents will be reading along by choice.

🌱

Teachable Moments

Each book models a core value — kindness, curiosity, perseverance — woven naturally into the plot, never preachy.

🔤

Age-Right Language

Written for independent readers and read-alouds alike. Vocabulary stretches just enough to grow with the child.

🔁

Re-readable by Design

Hidden details in the illustrations, callbacks to earlier pages, and character moments that reward returning readers.
